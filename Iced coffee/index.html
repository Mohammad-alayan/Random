  • nescafe

  • suger

Vietnamese Iced Coffee

Method

  1. Add 1 tsp of NESCAFÉ® Red Mug
  2. 100 ml hot water into a jug
  3. Pour 1 tbsp condensed milk into the jug and stir well
  4. Add ice cubes into a 300 ml glass. Pour the coffee mixture into the glass. Enjoy!
